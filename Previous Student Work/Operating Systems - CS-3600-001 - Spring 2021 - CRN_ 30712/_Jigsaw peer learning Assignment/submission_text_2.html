I learned that paging is placing a fixed-size of block of logical address space that is linked in physical address space by a page table. Segmentation is variable-sized blocks of logical address space all of the components of a program into a single address space by using a segmentation table. The advantages of both can be combined by using two tables to map pages into the physical memory.
